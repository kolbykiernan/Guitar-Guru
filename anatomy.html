This is the same alphabet that is used with piano, violin, and most instruments. Fortunatley for us, there are similarities with the English alphabet.

The Musical alphabet is made up of 12 notes - A, B, C, D, E, F, G, which can be seen on the white keys, plus 5 sharps, which can be seen with the # symbol on the black keys.

Now, notice how see each letter twice. That is becasue the musical alphabet continues to repeat itself from the lowest keys to the highest.

Type the letter "c" with your keyboard. Which key did we choose? What sound does it make?

Type the letter C, then D, then E all the way up until you reach the letter B. Notice how we are playing with the C through B on the left half of the keyboard
